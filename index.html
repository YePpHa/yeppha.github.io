What is YouTube Center?

YouTube Center is an extension for multiple browsers to enhance your experience on YouTube. It includes a lot of features including resizing the YouTube player, download videos, repeat videos and many, many more features.

Make the player glow as the video is playing.

Change the player size as you like.

Set your preferred resolution.

Download YouTube videos to your PC.
